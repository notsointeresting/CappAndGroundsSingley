[ Who we are ]

At the Capp and Grounds our philosophy is to provide students and faculty with high quality, low cost espresso drinks that can be enjoyed with friends or on the go, while providing a genuine coffee house feel.

Low Cost

We aim to provide students coffee at a reasonable price in order to help them perform to their maximum potential

High Quality

We only use locally roasted beans and manually steam and foam our milk in order to ensure high quality drinks

Internships

Our focus is to provide students culinary internship opportunity's right on campus

[Pictures]

Capp and Grounds is a creative, educational coffee service company aimed at offering high-quality, affordable espresso beverages using only fresh, locally roasted coffee beans. We are friends to our customers, employees, and students. We aim to become a district-wide recognized brand in the interest of providing students career learning and internship opportunities directly in their school. Our goal is district-wide expansion, continuing business education and maintaining a high quality level of service.
